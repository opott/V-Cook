30 Minute Halloumi Curry

Information

Serves 4

Prep time: 5min

Cook time: 25min

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 250g halloumi cheese
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced into strips)
  • 1 yellow onion (small, finely chopped)
  • 4 garlic cloves (grated)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 Tbsp Thai curry paste
  • 2 Tbsp double concentrated tomato paste
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 400g chickpeas (drained)
  • 3 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 400g full fat coconut milk
  • cilantro (chopped, for serving)

Methods

  1. Get your prep done before you start cooking. Deseed and slice the bell pepper into thin strips. Cut halloumi into bite-sized pieces. Finely chop the onion, grate the ginger and garlic cloves. Chop the cilantro.
  2. In a skillet or pan, heat oil over medium. Add halloumi and sear until golden all over, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from skillet.
  3. Add bell pepper and cook for about 5 minutes, or until it starts to soften. Add onion, garlic and ginger. Cook for 2 minutes. Stir in chili flakes, tomato paste and red curry paste. Add crushed tomatoes, chickpeas and soy sauce. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. You can now cook your rice according the package instructions, if using.
  5. Stir in the halloumi and simmer for 1 minute to warm through. Serve over rice, garnished with cilantro. Pair with a cucumber salad. Enjoy!
