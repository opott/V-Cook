World's Easiest Salsa

Information

Serves 4

Prep time: 10min

Ingredients

  • 790g can whole tomatoes
  • 200g can green chillies
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 green onions (chopped)
  • 1tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2tsp red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 4g cilantro
  • salt
  • black pepper

Methods

  1. Remove just the tomatoes from the can of whole tomatoes, place in a medium sized non-reactive mixing bowl. Using your fingers, or a fork and a sharp small knife, shred or break up the tomatoes.
  2. Mix in chopped green chiles, green onions, garlic (or garlic salt), olive oil and vinegar.
  3. Add back in about 1/4 cup of the tomato sauce from the can of whole tomatoes.
  4. Sprinkle with about 1/4 teaspoon of dried oregano. Mix in and taste. Adjust if needed. Add cilantro, and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Refrigerate when not using. Will keep several days.
