Potato Pillows

Information

Serves 4

Prep time: 10min

Cook time: 1hr

Ingredients

  • 330g potatoes
  • 2tbsp flour
  • 1tbsp corn starch
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of paprika
  • vegetable oil (to fry)

Methods

  1. Boil your potatoes for about 45 mins or until soft, drain and remove the skin.
  2. In a bowl, mash your potatoes, add flour, corn starch and a pinch of salt and paprika.
  3. Mix everything until smooth. Depending on the potatoes you use, the dough could be a bit sticky, but that’s ok.
  4. You can add a tiny more extra flour, but don’t add too much flour otherwise you’ll end up make gnocchi instead!
  5. Shape the dough into a 2.50cm (1 inch) snake and cut into a 3cm (1.5 inch) sections.
  6. Press the tines of a fork into each section of potato to create a ridged pattern.
  7. Heat vegetable oil to 170C/350F and fry your potato pillows for about 5/6 mins or until crispy.
  8. Drain and serve with some more salt and paprika.
