Pesto Potato Salad

Information

Serves 2

Prep time: 15min

Cook time: 20min

Ingredients

  • 230g potatoes
  • 35g fresh basic leaves
  • 40g walnuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Methods

  1. In a pot add potatoes, cover with water and boil on medium heat for 20 mins or till potatoes are cooked.
  2. In meantime prepare pesto by blending basil leaves, walnuts, garlic cloves, juice from one lemon, olive oil, salt & pepper. Coarse blend for a little crunch from walnuts.
  3. When potatoes are well cooked, drain water, remove skin of potatoes if not using organic and transfer to a mixing bowl. Add prepared pesto to potatoes and mix together, adjust salt and pepper as needed.
  4. Add seasoned croutons before serve if needed.
