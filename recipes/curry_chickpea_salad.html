Curried Chickpea Salad

Information

Serves 4

Prep time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas (drained)
  • 1/2 red onion (finely diced)
  • 1 celery stalk (finely diced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (cut into dices)
  • 2 Tbsp fresh herbs (chopped)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 100g greek youghurt
  • 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp curry powder
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard

Methods

  1. Prepare the curry yogurt dressing by mixing all dressing ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Add the chickpeas to a large bowl. Then mash them partially (optional) with a fork or a potato masher.
  3. Add the remaining salad ingredients and the dressing to the chickpeas. Season with salt and pepper, then stir until everything is well-combined. Serve and enjoy!
